+ Q17. Is the cybercrime portal safe and private?

Yes. It's an official Government of India platform with strong encryption. Your data is shared only with authorized agencies investigating the case.

üß± The Wall (Encryption)
+ Q18. Can I file a report if I don't have much proof?

Yes. You can still file a complaint, but screenshots, URLs, or receipts help in quicker action. Even partial evidence can help locate the fraud source.

+ Q19. How will I know if my complaint was submitted successfully?

You'll get an on-screen confirmation message. An SMS & email with the complaint ID will be sent (if using "Report & Track"). You can log in anytime to follow up.

+ Q20. How long does it take for police to respond?

It depends on case type. Financial frauds and crimes against women/children are prioritized. For urgent money-related scams, also call the 1930 helpline immediately.

+ Q21. Can I report without going to the police station?

Yes! You can file and track everything online via the portal ‚Äî no physical visit needed.

+ Q22. Whom can I contact for follow-up or escalation?

You can contact your State Nodal Officer (listed on the portal) for escalations or clarifications. These officers coordinate between citizens and cyber police.
